Data:
Non-trivial zeros of the Riemann zeta function from standard published tables (e.g. Odlyzko).

Method:
Least-squares fitting of a quadratic correction over zeros 1–200.
Extrapolation test using the same coefficients over zeros 501–700.

Result:
Polynomial correction is finite-range only.
The Riemann–von Mangoldt leading term is universal.
